Title: Show Countdown Timer
----
URL-Key: show-countdown-timer
----
Cat: PI
----
Steps:


1:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Click into the Session select field in the header bar and select a session from the list of available sessions.
	Details: &rarr; The session is loaded.
2:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Go to  **View &rarr; Countdown timer**.
	Details: >
		&rarr; The countdown timer settings dialog is opened.

3:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Set the Countdown timer and click on the **Start** button. 
	Details: >
		&rarr; The Countdown timer is opened as an overlay and starts to count down.
4:
	Subheader: 
	Action: 
	Details: &rarr; When the timer has run down, the display begins to blink red.
		
5:
	Subheader: 
	Action: To close the overlay when finished, hit the ESC key to close the overlay.
	Details: >
		The overlay is closed.	

6:
	Subheader: 
	Action: You are back in *Session* view.
	Details:
