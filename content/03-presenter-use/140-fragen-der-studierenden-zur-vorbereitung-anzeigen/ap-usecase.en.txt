Title: Present students' questions for preparation
----
URL-Key: present-student-questions-for-preparation
----
Cat: JiTT
----
Steps:

		
1:
	Subheader: 
	Action:  Click into the Session dropdown menu in the header bar and select a session from the list of available session.
	Details: &rarr; The session is loaded.

2:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Go to  **Mode &rarr;  Assignments (JiTT)** .
	Details: &rarr; Presenter is opened in mode *Assignments*.
	
3:
	Subheader: 
	Action: In the students' pane, the students' questions are listed.
	Details:  A black colored question indicates that a the questions has already been viewed while red colored questions indicate questions that have not been viewed yet.
		
4:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Click on a question.
	Details: >
		&rarr; The text of the question is shown.

5:
	Subheader:  Delete Question
	Action:>
		To delete a question once it is answered, click on the red **x** in the upper right corner.  **Note**: You can delete questions in both opened and closed states.
	Details: &rarr; A dialog is opened.

6:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Confirm that you want to delete the question by clicking the **Delete** button. 
	Details: &rarr; The question will be deleted.

7:
	Subheader: 
	Action: The question was deleted.
	Details:
