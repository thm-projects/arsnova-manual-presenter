Title: Edit questions
----
URL-Key: edit-questions
----
Cat: Edit
----
Steps:

1:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Click into the Session dropdown menu in the header bar and select a session from the list of available session. 
	Details: &rarr; The session is loaded.

2:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Go to  **Mode &rarr; Editing**.
	Details: >
		&rarr; You get into editing mode. 		
3:
	Subheader: 
	Action: In the lecturer pane, click one of tabs **Questions for lecture** or **Questions for preparation** depending on the type of question you want to edit. 
	Details: &rarr; A list of all available questions is shown.	

4:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Click on the question you want to edit.
	Details:>
		The question is opened in the students' pane.
		
		
		
5:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Edit your changes.
	Details: >
		
		**Note**: The question format cannot be changed.

6:
	Subheader: 
	Action: When ready, click **Save**.	
	Details: &rarr; If the question was saved successfully, a success message is shown.
