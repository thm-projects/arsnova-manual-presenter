Title: Edit questions
----
URL-Key: edit-questions
----
Cat: Edit
----
Steps:

1:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Select a session from the dropdown menu in the header bar.
	Details: &rarr; The session is opened.

2:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Click on  **Mode &rarr; Edit**.
	Details: >
		&rarr; You are directed to editing mode. 		
3:
	Subheader: 
	Action: In the lecturer pane, click one of tabs **Lecturer questions** or **Preparation Questions** depending on the type of question you want to edit. 
	Details: &rarr; A list of all available questions is shown.	

4:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Click on the question you want to edit.
	Details:>
		The question is opened in the audience pane.
		
		
		
5:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Make your changes.
	Details: >
		
		**Note**: The question format cannot be changed.

6:
	Subheader: 
	Action: When ready, click **Save**.	
	Details: &rarr; If the question was saved successfully, a success message is shown.
