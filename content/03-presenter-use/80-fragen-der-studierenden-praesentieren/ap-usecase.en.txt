Title:  Present/delete questions from audience
----
URL-Key: present-audience-questions
----
Cat: LF
----
Steps:

		
1:
	Subheader:  Present Questions
	Action: Select a session from the dropdown menu in the header bar.
	Details: &rarr; The session is opened.

2:
	Subheader: 
	Action: In the audience pane, click on the tab **Audience Questions**. If the tab is not active, the number of new questions is shown in real time, e.g. (+1) if one new question is available. 
	Details:  &rarr; All available audience questions are shown. 
		
3:
	Subheader:  Show a Question
	Action: The questions are shown in the order in which they were asked. Questions that have not been viewed yet are shown in red. To show a question, click on it. 
	Details: &rarr; The complete question is shown.
			
4:
	Subheader:  Delete Questions
	Action:>
		To delete a question once it was answered, click on the red **x**. **Note**: You can delete a question in both opened and closed states.
	Details: &rarr; A dialog opens.

5:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Confirm the deletion of the question question by clicking on  **Delete**: &rarr; The question is deleted.	

6:
	Subheader: 
	Action: The question was deleted.
	Details:
